About Me

I'm Feza. I'm actually an electrical-electronics engineer. But art has always been my biggest passion. While I was still studying engineering, I taught myself digital design, and since that day, I haven't stopped designing, experimenting, and creating. I work across a wide range: from brand identities to UI/UX, from game design to user experience, from illustration to digital products.

I've worked on many different projects, collaborated with different teams, taught design at university, and merged my technical background with creativity. Yes, I'm an engineer-designer. Yes, my story is a bit unusual, but it comes down to passion.

In short: I design everything.
